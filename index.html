About Me

Wholesaler And Retailer of School And College Uniform

Established in 1981, Porwal stands as a market leader in the uniform fabrics. One out of six school going child wears a uniform stitched with our fabric. With 700 authorised distributors globally, our products are available across 15,000 retail outlets both nationally & internationally. The Porwal Group is driven by the vision of building a sustainable, profitable, growth oriented and socially responsible conglomerate through strong and effective systems, committed teams and satisfied stakeholders. Today, the Porwal group is recognised as a “System Oriented Management Supported” corporate.
